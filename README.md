## Problem Statement
The goal of this analysis is to identify how many times students from each school logged into the school portal during the current school year. This helps measure student engagement and identify schools that may require additional support or intervention.

## Dataset
The dataset contains portal login records of students, including school names and the number of login occurrences per student. Data was provided in Excel format and anonymized for analysis purposes.

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Basic data cleaning techniques

## Analysis Process
- Reviewed and cleaned the dataset to ensure consistent school names
- Aggregated student login counts by school
- Summarized total login frequency per school
- Identified schools with high and low portal engagement

## Key Findings
- Login activity varied significantly across schools
- Some schools showed consistently high portal usage, indicating strong engagement
- Other schools recorded low login frequency, suggesting possible access or awareness issues

## Recommendations
- Provide additional orientation or support for schools with low login activity
- Encourage regular portal use through school-level initiatives
- Monitor login trends monthly to track improvements
